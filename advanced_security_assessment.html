Risk Calculation Methodology

How We Calculate Security Risk

1. Data Breach Cost

Base Cost = Revenue × 4% (capped at $5M AUD)

Based on IBM Cost of Data Breach reports and Australian OAIC breach notifications. Reflects direct costs of incident response, legal fees, notifications, and immediate business disruption.

2. Compliance Fines

Fines = $250K base × Industry Multiplier (if revenue > $3M)

Based on Privacy Act mandatory breach notification requirements and typical ACMA/OAIC penalties. Higher for regulated industries (healthcare 4x, financial 5x).

3. Productivity Loss

Productivity = Employees × $2,000 × Industry Factor

Annual productivity impact from security incidents, including incident response time, system downtime, and recovery efforts. Based on Ponemon Institute research.

4. Reputation Impact

Reputation = Revenue × 2% × Industry Factor

Business impact from customer churn, lost deals, and marketing recovery costs following security incidents.

5. Risk Adjustment Factors

Security Maturity: Basic (1.0x), Partial (0.7x), Good (0.4x), Advanced (0.2x)

M365 Licensing: E5 (0.8x), E3 (1.2x), Business Premium (1.4x)

Feature Implementation: Each missing critical feature adds 5-15% risk
